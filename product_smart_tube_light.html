Intellizens Smart Tube Light (ISTL)

Intelligent tube lighting for large-scale manufacturing and commercial interiors.

USE CASE

Delivers intelligent, energy-aware illumination for high-occupancy work areas using automated scheduling, motion sensing, and lux-based dimming to maintain optimal brightness while minimizing energy use. Remote control and real-time monitoring via a centralized dashboard provide quick adjustments, centralized oversight, and rapid fault detection across multiple benches or zones. Integrated analytics monitor power consumption, LED aging, and maintenance needs, enabling proactive maintenance and cost-effective lighting deployment facility-wide.

BENEFITS
     
  • Provides precise, per-zone lighting control combined with energy analytics to reduce operational costs and enhance facility reliability.

KEY FEATURES

Automated scheduling and auto-intensity adjustment deliver right brightness at the right time.

Over-temperature protection enhances safety and extends LED lifespan.

Remote control and real-time monitoring enabled by LoRaWAN simplify facility-wide management.

Performance analytics for power usage, operational patterns, and component health.

Compact, durable design suitable for offices, production lines, and commercial interiors.
