INTELLIZENS Smart Meter

Industrial‑grade, remotely managed energy intelligence.

USE CASE

Real-time three-phase energy measurement with remote visibility into readings, status, and settings enables precise billing and rapid fault isolation across distribution zones. Per-area energy monitoring allows utilities and large end users to track consumption patterns, peak loads, and grid stability by distribution junction. ML-driven analytics convert energy data into actionable insights for demand management, outage prevention, and enhanced grid reliability.

BENEFITS:
     
  • Immediate detection of energy deviations and anomalies for faster response and safer operations.
    •  
  • Per-zone load insights, automated reporting, and threshold-based alerts drive demand management and reduce downtime.
    •  
  • Scalable integration with SCADA/BMS and existing analytics systems supports secure, enterprise-wide energy intelligence.
    •  
  • Measurable improvements in TPM and OEE across multi-site deployments.

KEY FEATURES

Remote monitoring and control for real-time access to meter data and settings from anywhere.

Continuous measurement of voltage, current, power, power factor, frequency, and energy parameters for accurate consumption tracking.

Automated reports to visualize usage patterns, peak loads, and efficiency opportunities.

Optional configurable alerts for consumption thresholds, over-current, voltage anomalies, and missed communications..

Industrial IIoT-ready with Wi-Fi, LoRa, LTE connectivity, and Modbus RTU/TCP gateway support for SCADA/BMS integration.

Designed for DIN-rail panel installation with external split-core CTs and tamper-evident seals.

Operates in industrial temperature ranges with surge-protected inputs (model dependent).

Compatible with Evoluzn IIoT platform and exportable to enterprise energy management tools.

Smart Advantages

Combines accurate 3-phase measurement, remote management, and ML-driven analytics to optimize energy costs and improve grid reliability. Flexible connectivity and alerting options make it ideal for plant-wide rollouts and seamless SCADA/BMS integration.
