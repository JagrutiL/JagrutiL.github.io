INTELLIZENS Programmable Load Controller

Centralized industrial control with intelligent monitoring and automated regulation.

USE CASE

Programs industrial workshops with real-time control over energy-intensive machinery to optimize load distribution, minimize peak tariff penalties, and ensure safe, cost-effective production. Continuously monitors total load and detects peak thresholds using programmable logic, automatically switching machines ON/OFF based on pre-set load limits, tariff windows, and production priorities.

BENEFITS
     
  • Smart scheduling aligns machine operation with energy tariff windows for cost savings.
    •  
  • Prevents overload trips and penalties by maintaining load within safe thresholds.
    •  
  • Functions as a fail-safe system protecting sensitive equipment and supporting emergency shutdowns.
    •  
  • Manual override and compliance support ensure safety and full operator control during critical operations.

KEY FEATURES

Manual pushbutton control plus seamless remote operation and monitoring via centralized IoT dashboard for flexibility.

Automated temperature regulation adapts control dynamically based on sensor feedback for enhanced safety and efficiency.

Detailed power consumption reporting reveals energy usage patterns to support cost management.

Remote dashboard access enables real-time system adjustment and fault detection, reducing onsite visits.

Rugged design built for industrial environments, fully integrable with Intellizens IIoT ecosystem.

Smart Advantages

Combines manual simplicity with intelligent automation and remote monitoring. Integrated temperature control and energy analytics empower operators to optimize plant performance, reduce downtime, and minimize energy waste—ideal for complex multi-machine setups requiring centralized, data-driven IIoT management.
