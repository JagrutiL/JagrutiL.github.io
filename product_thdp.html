INTELLIZENS SMART THDP MONITOR (ISTHDP)

Integrated environmental sensing for controlled spaces.

USE CASE

Automates scheduling and remote oversight for continuous monitoring of temperature, humidity, and differential pressure in controlled environments. Enables rapid fault detection and proactive environmental management via adaptive alerts and a centralized dashboard. Durable hardware and seamless integration into IIoT ecosystems support scalable multi-room or multi-facility deployment where HVAC performance and air quality are critical.

BENEFITS:
     
  • Provides comprehensive visibility into critical environmental conditions for faster deviation response and reduced downtime.
    •  
  • Supports maintenance efficiency and energy savings through proactive alerts, trend analytics, and remote control
    •  
  • Helps ensure compliance with stringent cleanroom and industrial environmental standards.

KEY FEATURES

Automated scheduling with remote monitoring enables operation only when necessary and control from anywhere.

Continuous measurement of temperature, humidity, and differential pressure for broad environmental insight.

Advanced alert system for over/under temperature, humidity, and pressure thresholds.

Modern backlit LCD display offers clear multi-parameter real-time visualization.

Rugged design suitable for industrial, cleanroom, and controlled environment applications.
