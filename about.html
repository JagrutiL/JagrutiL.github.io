A Journey of Resilience, Vision, and Ownership

Evoluzn’s story began amid transformation and challenge. After leading high-stakes wireless network divisions at multinational technology firms, the founder witnessed how strategic shifts can disrupt trusted customer relationships. This inspired a bold commitment: to build a company that stands unwaveringly by its customers and owns the technology it delivers, regardless of market tides.

Founded in 2012 with limited resources but immense determination, Evoluzn weathered years of persistence—self-funded and evolving from trading margins to deep investment in innovation. Our journey is marked by hard-earned lessons and an unwavering focus on addressing industrial challenges through advanced IoT and high-precision cleanroom technologies, essential for modern manufacturing quality and operational excellence.

Today, Evoluzn is more than a technology provider—we are a partner in transformation, driven by shared growth, innovation, and impact. Our mission is clear: to deliver enduring solutions, support our customers consistently, and create pathways of ownership and opportunity for every team member.

FOUNDER & CEO
Suryanarayana Venkata Jonnalagadda (SURYA)
With over 30 years of global leadership in telecommunications and technology, Surya progressed from Cellular Field Engineer (GSM) to spearheading major network deployments and regional operations at Motorola. He successfully built and scaled profitable WiMAX and LTE device business units before founding Evoluzn Inc. in 2012, a Chicago-based broadband device company. In 2019, he bootstrapped Evoluzn India Private Limited, focusing on Industrial IoT and cleanroom technologies. Surya holds an MBA from DePaul University, is Six Sigma Green Belt certified, and has a Master's in Program Management—combining deep technical expertise with strategic business leadership.
