INTELLIZENS WIRE TEMP SYNC (IWTS)

Real-time temperature intelligence for safer, smarter operations

USE CASE

Real-time monitoring of critical wires and bus bars to eliminate costly thermal cameras, drastically reduce fire risks, and prevent unplanned downtime in electrical panels and production lines.

BENEFITS:
     
  • Enhances safety and compliance by preventing overheating incidents.
    •  
  • Enables rapid incident response through proactive temperature monitoring with customizable thresholds.
    •  
  • Reduces downtime and protects critical assets with local and remote actionable alerts.

KEY FEATURES

Continuous, real-time temperature tracking of wires and conductors.

Customizable threshold alerts for abnormal temperature variations.

Historical data visualization for predictive maintenance insights.

Integrated audible hooter alarm for immediate critical event notification.

Suitable for power panels, machinery cabling, and precision production environments.

Smart Advantages

Combines instant temperature tracking with smart alerting and analytics through an easy-to-use interface, historical data insights, and integrated alarms to help maintenance teams act proactively and improve operational reliability.
