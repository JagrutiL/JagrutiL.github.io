Intellizens Hi-Bay Light (IHBL)

Bright, efficient, and intelligent industrial illumination.

USE CASE

Delivers high-brightness, energy-efficient lighting for high-ceiling industrial and commercial spaces. Features automated scheduling and occupancy-aware dimming to provide optimal light levels where work occurs. Remote monitoring and alerting enable centralized oversight, quick fault detection, and proactive maintenance across multiple high-bay zones. Rugged design and IoT connectivity facilitate seamless integration with existing IIoT and building management platforms.

BENEFITS
     
  • Achieves up to 70% energy savings with high-lumen LEDs and intelligent dimming.
    •  
  • Enhances worker safety and productivity by maintaining consistent lighting and rapid alerting of performance issues.
    •  
  • Reduces maintenance costs through dual LED drivers and predictive monitoring.
    •  
  • Scalable for multi-site deployment with integration into broader lighting and energy management ecosystems.

KEY FEATURES

High-lumen LEDs deliver maximum brightness and efficiency (up to 160 lm/W).

Dual LED drivers ensure enhanced reliability and longer service life.

Built-in motion and ambient light sensors enable automatic light level adjustments.

Remote monitoring and performance alerting simplify facility management.

Advanced heat dissipation and rugged housing designed for industrial environments.
